## 📰 Story 1: Kids Around the World Are Learning to Code!

### 💡 What Happened?
Children in many countries are learning computer coding in school. One of the first things they learn is a command called **"git add ."** It tells the computer: *"Save everything I worked on so I can share it!"* Schools in Turkey, Malaysia, and Nigeria are now teaching this to kids as young as 7!

### 🌍 Why It Matters?
When children learn to code, they can **build apps, websites, and tools** that help their families and communities. These skills help kids find good jobs and solve real problems when they grow up.

### ☪️ What Islam Teaches Us?
The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ said:
> *"Seeking knowledge is an obligation upon every Muslim."*
> *(Ibn Majah)*

Learning — whether it is Arabic, maths, or coding — is an act of **worship** when we use it to help others. Allah loves those who use their minds!

### 🤲 What Can We Do?
- Ask a parent or teacher about free coding websites like **Scratch** or **Code.org**
- Practice saving your schoolwork carefully — just like "git add ." saves computer work!
- Make **du'a** that Allah blesses your learning

## 📰 Story 2: Young Muslim Coders Build a Qur'an App!

### 💡 What Happened?
A team of young Muslim teenagers from Canada built a **free Qur'an learning app** for children. They used coding tools — including the "git add ." command — to save their work every single day for six months. They worked together, fixed their mistakes, and never gave up. Now thousands of children use their app!

### 🌍 Why It Matters?
This shows that **young people can create amazing things** when they work hard and work together. You do not have to be an adult to make something that helps the whole Ummah!

### ☪️ What Islam Teaches Us?
Allah says in the Qur'an:
> *"Indeed, Allah will not change the condition of a people until they change what is in themselves."*
> *(Surah Ar-Ra'd 13:11)*

When we put in **effort and trust Allah**, great things can happen. These teenagers changed their skills — and then changed lives!

### 🤲 What Can We Do?
- Think of **one problem** in your community — could technology help fix it?
- Work on a project with a friend or sibling — teamwork is a Sunnah!
- Thank Allah for the talents He gave you 🤲

## 📰 Story 3: "Git Add ." Teaches Us About Teamwork!

### 💡 What Happened?
Did you know that **"git add ."** is part of a bigger system called **Git**? Git lets many people work on the **same project at the same time** without making a mess! Coders all over the world — from Egypt to Indonesia to Brazil — use it every day to build things *together*, even if they have never met!

### 🌍 Why It Matters?
The internet and the apps we love were built by **thousands of people working as one team**. No single person could do it alone. Cooperation makes big, beautiful things possible!

### ☪️ What Islam Teaches Us?
The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ said:
> *"The believers in their mutual kindness, compassion, and sympathy are just like one body."*
> *(Bukhari & Muslim)*

The Ummah is like one body — and one **great team**! When we help each other and share our gifts, we become stronger together. 💪

### 🤲 What Can We Do?
- Practice **sharing** your ideas and listening to others' ideas too
- Help a classmate with something they find difficult
- Remember: even small contributions matter — just like every "git add ." adds up to a finished project! 🌟
